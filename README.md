# LegitCheck

LegitCheck is a web platform built for sneakerheads, collectors, and streetwear enthusiasts who want confidence in the authenticity of their items.

Problem:
The modern resale market is flooded with counterfeit goods.
Buyers and collectors often face the same challenges:

• No quick way to verify authenticity
• Lack of transparent product history
• No unified system to track origin, serial numbers, or ownership
• High risk when buying from secondary marketplaces

Solution:
LegitCheck solves this by providing a structured and community-driven verification system.

Our platform allows users to:
- Submit items for legit checks (clothing, sneakers, accessories)
- Receive feedback from experienced community members
- Build a digital authenticity profile for each item


LegitCheck is built around the idea that every product should have a story you can trust.
In a market overwhelmed by counterfeits, we aim to introduce transparency by giving each item a digital identity.
Instead of relying on guesswork, users can access structured information about a product from its origin and characteristics to community-driven verification. Over time, each item evolves into a traceable entity with its own history, shaped by real users and real checks.This approach transforms authentication from a one-time action into an ongoing, reliable process powered by both technology and community.

Our goal is to make authenticity verification simple, accessible, and trustworthy for everyone.
